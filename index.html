Hello, I'm Hoang Pham.

I will be joining the College of Computing and Data Science at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) as a CS Ph.D. Student in Spring 2026, under the advisement of Prof. Luu Anh Tuan.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Hanoi University of Science and Technology. Before graduate school, I spent three years at Viettel AI conducting NLP research, working closely with Dr. Bui Khac Hoai Nam.

My research interests focus on foundation models and their applications, especially interpretable and trustworthy NLP systems that tackle challenges such as outdated knowledge, inconsistent reasoning, limited explainability, and multi-modality. Beyond research, I enjoy solution architecture and building scalable, real-world systems, often integrating machine learning components.
